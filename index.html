Pedri

Pedri is a Spanish midfielder for FC Barcelona, known for his exceptional vision, ball control, and intelligence. Often called the "new Iniesta," he controls the game with smooth passing and tireless movement. Despite his young age, he’s already a key playmaker for both club and country.


Individual Honors
1. Golden Boy Award(2021)
2. Kopa Trophy(2021)
3. UEFA Euro 2020 Young Player of the Tournament
4. UEFA Team of the Tournament - Euro 2020

Play Style
Pedri plays with calm and clarity, always thinking a step ahead. His movement is smooth, his passing sharp — everything feels effortless. He’s the kind of player who makes the game look simple, even when it’s not. Under pressure, he stays cool and finds space where no one else can. Quiet on the surface, but always in control.
